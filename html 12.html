About us

History- "better eats " was created in 2021 by Tola Showunmi and Mark Hughes .The main aim of the company was to provide a solution to the food industry is it possible to have cheap, healthy and organic ready to go meals.Our goal is to be able to get this meal is as many improvised areas and school. Why use us -Our company is solving a problem that the rest of the food industry has avoided , which is selling quality food for affordable prices .We as a company believe all children deserve access to quality food and due to our affordable prices , more children across America will have this access.
